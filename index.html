EU Parliament vote on the 5th of July 2018 regarding the current EU copyright reform proposal

In this vote, the European Parliament had to decide whether to confirm the Committee position on controversial Articles 11 and 13 of the proposal.

Article 11 concerns the creation of a "link tax", imposing anyone using snippets (including link preview) of journalistic content to obtain a license from the publisher.

Article 13 would impose to hosting platforms new preemptive filtering obligations for copyrighted content.
